Description

Summary of the Maid as observed (click to open)

The maid is a petite unfinished cyborg, created initially for clearing out trash and leaving no traces behind. The creator's whereabouts remain unknown and is implied to have suffered the same fate as all who where unfortunate enough to cross her path.

The maid uses her mouth like a vacuum to suck things into her belly, more often than not, against her will or without prior knowledge of her actions resulting in seemingly accidental scenarios. After a very short time, the contents compress down. Things can only enter her system through her mouth, and exit out through her derriere. Speaking of which, this seems to always result in forceful farts.

Compressing down contents seems to convert mass on to her frame, making the problem worse as the larger she becomes, the more potent her abilities.

Despite her nightmare-fuel implications, her first instinct is to keep a sense of normality by being positive and adopting an assertive attitude. Despite an unsatiable hunger, she does not resort to fill her belly whenever she has the chance. One can make the argument she does things to help man-kind, but something always seems to go wrong, ending in the unfortunate disappearence of anything in front of her.

Curious to note, aside from her primary function of disposal, the maid was slated to be mass produced and earn the creator heaps of money, by creating copies of itself - after each successful job. Each new copy better than the last as they add new modules/components missing from its predecessor.

Upon investigation of the creator's abode, it has been found that the original copy has the following missing components;

  • Speech: the ability to speak like a human
    •
  • Smart gadget management: the ability to know when a module has been altered, such as knowing that she has been tampered while being distracted
  • Frame miniaturisation: the ability to rescale herself without losing any of the gains
  • Selective inventory reproduction: the ability to selectively reproduce anything she has compressed down at will, such as a VIP
  • Strength accumulation: the ability to accumulate added strength to support herself better, by default she feels like a lanky 5'6" woman
  • Smart Expulsion control: the ability to temper the potency / decide whatever comes out the back, if at all
  • Smart Chamber control: the ability to know if any jams / blockages occur during compression, resulting in less efficiency
