Description

Summary of Makana as observed (click to open)

Makana is an unfinished cyborg dressed in a suggestive French maid's outfit.

Makana was created initially for clearing out trash and leaving no traces behind.

Her creator's whereabouts remain unknown and is implied to have suffered the same fate as all who were unfortunate enough to cross her path.

Makana uses her mouth like a vacuum to suck things down into her belly. More often than not, this happens against her will or without knowing this will result in accidental consumption of people.

After a very short time, whatever is in her belly gets compressed down.

Things can only enter her system through her mouth, and exit out through her derriere. This always results in forceful farts, reeking of whatever she just compressed.

Compressing down contents seems to add mass on to her (at first) petite frame, making her abilities more potent.

Despite having an unsatiable hunger, Makana controls herself to only vacuum and swallow things which she believes she can clean.

One can make the argument she does things to help man-kind, but something always seems to go wrong, ending in the unfortunate disappearence of anything in front of her.

Makana's first instinct is to keep a sense of normality by being positive and adopting an assertive attitude to her 'job'.

Makana has a fixed amount of energy she can dedicate to her functions at any given time. Makana cannot use her vacuum while keeping her vision up.

Upon further investigation, it has been found that Makana has the following missing components;

  • A tongue & teeth: Makana has no tongue and no teeth, so she cannot chew or taste like ordinary people. Inside her mouth is pitch black. Her speech has a faint, synthesized effect to it.
  • Smart gadget management: the ability to know when a module has been altered, such as knowing that she has been tampered while being distracted
  • Frame miniaturisation: the ability to rescale herself without losing any of her mass.
  • Selective inventory reproduction: the ability to selectively reproduce anything she has compressed down at will, such as a VIP
  • Strength accumulation: the ability to accumulate strength to support herself better. By default she feels like a lanky 5'6" woman
  • Smart Expulsion control: the ability to temper the potency / decide whatever comes out the back, if at all
  • Smart Chamber control: the ability to know if any jams / blockages occur during compression, resulting in inconsistant efficiency.
